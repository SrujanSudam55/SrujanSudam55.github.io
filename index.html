How I think and work

Strategy-led work, shaped by deep exposure to analytics, AI, and real operating constraints. I work at decision level, but my thinking is sharpened by having lived inside the machinery.

Strategy and judgment I focus on framing the right decision under uncertainty. That means making trade-offs explicit, defining ownership, and being clear about what “good” looks like before solutions enter the room.
Analytical and AI leverage My strategy work is informed by hands-on experience with data, analytics, and AI. I use models and systems as thinking tools to test assumptions, spot failure modes early, and separate signal from noise.
From insight to operating reality I design for what survives after the pilot. Adoption, governance, edge cases, and incentives are treated as first-order concerns, not implementation details.
Frameworks I am developing a small set of decision frameworks that codify this way of working. Not templates. Practical tools for forcing clarity when choices are uncomfortable. Coming soon.
